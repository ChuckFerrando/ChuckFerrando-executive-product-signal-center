# AI Automated Research Signal

## Source
Aakash Gupta — PM’s Guide to Autoresearch (2026)

## Signal Summary

AI is transforming research from a manual activity into an automated, iterative system driven by prompts, evaluation frameworks, and agent-assisted workflows.

## Key Observations

- Research can now be:
  - automated
  - repeated at scale
  - continuously improved
- PMs can run:
  - multiple research iterations
  - prompt experiments
  - evaluation loops
- Autoresearch systems include:
  - structured prompts
  - evaluation criteria
  - iterative refinement
- Research is no longer constrained by:
  - time
  - manual synthesis
- AI enables parallel exploration of ideas

## Signal Implications

- Research becomes a system, not a task
- Speed of insight generation increases dramatically
- PMs who systematize research gain disproportionate advantage
- Evaluation frameworks become critical capability

## Classification

- Type: Workflow + Capability Shift
- Domain: AI Product Management
- Strength: Very High
- Actionability: Very High
